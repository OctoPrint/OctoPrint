--- layout: post author: foosel title: 'New release: 1.2.5' date: 2015-08-31 13:33:42 +0000 originalUrl: https://plus.google.com/+OctoprintOrg/posts/2V7ZfqkTgJ7 --- Mostly fixes of minor bugs and annoyances but also a couple of improvements:

  * The settings dialog now visualizes when settings are saving and when they are being retrieved
  * Better compatibility with the Repetier firmware
  * Easier configuration of the OctoPrint update mechanism in the Software Update Plugin (including hints in the UI on what to do)
  * Made disk space running low a bit more obvious through visual warning on configurable thresholds

Find the full changelog here: https://github.com/foosel/OctoPrint/releases/tag/1.2.5

If you don't get an "Update Now" button with your update notification, read this: https://github.com/foosel/OctoPrint/wiki/Plugin:-Software-Update#making-octoprint-updateable-on-existing-installations﻿
